Fashion

Haute couture. When we hear this word, we often think of surrealist clothes ... But in reality, when we ask ourselves more about this area, haute couture and ready-to-wear are above all an art. The art of assembling materials, colors. Each season, the artistic directors demonstrate flawless creativity and originality while keeping the DNA of their brand. I find it fascinating, watching the evolution of a haute couture house over time.

During the last fashion week in Paris (September - October 2021), one of the shows that struck me the most was that of Versace. The collection showcased beautiful colors, the overlays of fabrics / pieces were very well mastered as well as the fittings and the making. Donatella Versace really succeeded in taking us into her world, especially thanks to the sets and music.

Geopolitics

Geopolitics could be defined as a human science that studies politics and international relations based on geographic and historical facts. This science deals with international news by taking into account different actors. It allows us to better understand the world of today and tomorrow as well as these issues.

Moreover, geopolitics is everywhere! It punctuates our daily lives without even realizing it! At the base of geopolitics, there are two fundamental notions: hard power (is deployed in the form of coercion: using force, the threat of force, economic sanctions, or inducements of payment) and soft power (In contrast to the coercive nature of hard power, soft power describes the use of positive attraction and persuasion to achieve foreign policy objectives.).
